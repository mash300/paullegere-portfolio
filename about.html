Technical Product Designer

Designing scalable digital products at the intersection of systems, UX, and front-end development

Hi, I’m Paul! I’m a Technical Product Designer who bridges structured design and technical know-how. After 25+ years crafting visual systems for animation, I pivoted into product design with a full-stack web programming diploma (Honours).

I love solving real-world problems—whether that’s designing SaaS tools, collaborating with developers, or optimizing systems. My work aims to balance usability, technical constraints, and business impact.

Beyond design, I’m driven by belonging and making others’ lives easier. If you’re curious to chat, feel free to reach out below!
